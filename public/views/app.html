HOW TO PLAY


  1. Each player will always begin with and always have 10 cards
  2. For each round one player is chosen as the Card Czar
  3. Everyone else answers the black question card by clicking on the card they want to use
  4. The Card Czar then picks the favorite answer and whoever played that card wins that round
  5. Want to adorn your avatar with a glorious crown? donate to charity after the game!





Please remember to donate to save a child!
